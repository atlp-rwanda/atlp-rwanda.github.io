Great! How Can I Work With Them?

The best way to describe our service offering is Engineering As A Service. In essence, we augment your “in-house” engineering team, with more developers FOR FREE! For a period of three(3) months.

After the three(3) month tenure is concluded for a given Apprentice, you can go ahead to hire them or let them go and/or get even more technical talent on your team. Think of it as a Free Three Month Trial for awesome junior engineering talent!

What Support Do I get From Andela?

We are committed to the success of your company, as that ensures the success for our Apprentices. To that end, we provide support including:

  • Ensuring the Apprentice has the appropriate work tools,
  • Facilitating feedback to and from the Apprentice(s), to ensure they’re getting meaningful work done for your company,
  • Facilitating technical and professional skills coaching.

How Much does the service cost?

This service will cost you absolutely nothing! In essence you have nothing to lose, and everything to gain!

You'd also be contributing to the development of technical talent on the African continent. We’re able to staff new engineers every month at your discretion, and each engineer is expected to stay on the team for at least 3 months(12 weeks) after which you can either hire them or they roll off your team.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are Andela Apprentices, what skills do they possess?

They are talented engineers who’ve undergone intensive training in the PERN(PosgreSQL & MongoDB, Express. React, Node) software stack. As well as an immersive, experiential process of learning the soft skills required to thrive as a professional in any distributed engineering team.

How much does it cost to hire Andela Apprentices?

$0. Yes, absolutely nothing! It’s a win win. You get to try awesome engineering talent for free within a 3 month period and you can decide to hire them full time once they've proven to add value to your team. On our end, we get to continue upskilling the Apprentices until we believe they’re totally ready to join the job ecosystem.

How long would they be available to me for free?

For a period of 3 months after they start working with you. Afterwards, you have to either hire them or let them roll off your team gracefully.

Ok, but can I hire them directly if I like their work?

Of course! Once the 3 month period from when they start working with you is up, you can go on ahead to hire them immediately. If you’d like to hire sooner, and prefer not to continue the “3 month trial”, that’s also possible!

What support do I get from Andela when I hire these Apprentices?
  • Performance management. We provide a strong and tight feedback loop between the Apprentice and your Engineering manager/lead to make sure they’re fully aware of your expectations from them and act accordingly.
  • If it so happens that after weeks of feedback, you’re still unhappy with a particular Apprentice’s performance, we roll them off your team and replace them at your request.
  • We hold monthly technical workshops as well as coaching sessions, to make sure we’re able to impart them with more knowledge than they’re potentially exposed to on their engagements. Armed with such knowledge, they become more of an asset on your team, as they’re able to bring fresh ideas to old and new challenges alike.
What’s the difference between Andela and the Andela Technical Leadership Program?
  • Good question. Andela here refers to the company you know and love whose mission is to distribute as many available opportunities to all available talent, world wide. Specifically, to achieve this as a global company, we plan to create infrastructure that ensures we’re able to place the right talent at the right time for as many companies that need such talent.
  • However, Andela Rwanda has a responsibility to implement this mission by sourcing talent from within Africa and train them to become world class junior engineers.
  • The Andela Technical Leadership Program(ATLP) is the vehicle with which Andela Rwanda is making that happen. It consists of a 5 month intensive technical and soft skills training, a 3 month Apprenticeship with a reputable tech company and a 2 week career coaching program.
