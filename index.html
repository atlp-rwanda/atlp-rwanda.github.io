Who are andela Apprentices?

From day one, the trainees learn by doing. The 20 weeks of the training program simulates a real world technology product setup, where they learn to work within a team to build web applications with best practices, based on Javascript and it’s associated frameworks. Top candidates who are able to make it through the training program successfully, are then officially regarded as Apprentice Developers.

Great! How can I work with them?

The best way to describe our service offering is Engineering As A Service. In essence, we augment your “in-house” engineering team, with more developers for a period of 12 weeks.

After the 12 weeks tenure is concluded for a given Apprentice, you can go ahead to hire them or let them go and/or get even more technical talent on your team. Think of it as a 3 Months Trial for awesome junior engineering talents!

What support do I get from Andela?

We are committed to the success of your company, as that ensures the success for our Apprentices. To that end, we provide support including:

  • Performance management. We provide a strong and tight feedback loop between the Apprentice and your Engineering manager/lead to make sure they’re fully aware of your expectations from them and act accordingly.
  • We hold monthly technical workshops and profesional skills coaching sessions, to make sure we’re able to impart them with more knowledge than they’re potentially exposed to on their engagements. Armed with such knowledge, they become more of an asset on your team.

How much does the service cost?

This service is provided free of charge by Andela. However, we require companies to provide stipend to apprentices.

You will also be contributing to the development of technical talent on the African continent. Each developer is expected to stay on your team for at least 12 weeks after which you can either hire them or they roll off your team and we will recommend other full time opportunities for them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are Andela Apprentices, what skills do they possess?

They are talented developers who’ve undergone intensive training in the PERN(PosgreSQL & MongoDB, Express. React, Node) software stack. As well as an immersive, experiential process of learning the soft skills required to thrive as a professional in any distributed engineering team.

Ok, but can I hire them directly if I like their work?

Of course! Once the 12 weeks period from when they start working with you is up, you can go on ahead to hire them immediately. If you’d like to hire sooner, and prefer not to continue the “3 month trial”, that’s also possible!

What support do I get from Andela?
  • Performance management. We provide a strong and tight feedback loop between the Apprentice and your Engineering manager/lead to make sure they’re fully aware of your expectations from them and act accordingly.
  • We hold monthly technical workshops as well as coaching sessions, to make sure we’re able to impart them with more knowledge than they’re potentially exposed to on their engagements. Armed with such knowledge, they become more of an asset on your team.
What’s the difference between Andela and the Andela Technical Leadership Program?
  • Good question. Andela here refers to the company you know and love whose mission is to distribute as many available opportunities to all available talent, world wide. Specifically, to achieve this as a global company, we plan to create infrastructure that ensures we’re able to place the right talent at the right time for as many companies that need such talent.
  • However, Andela Rwanda has a responsibility to implement this mission by sourcing talent from within Africa and train them to become job ready developers. The Andela Technical Leadership Program (ATLP) is the vehicle with which Andela Rwanda is making that happen.
